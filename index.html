How to use Muno

Add words — tap the + button to add new words with definitions.

Organize — create folders to group words by topic or language.

Practice — tap "Begin practice" to review flashcards. Swipe right or tap "Mark as known" when you know a word.

Track progress — the progress ring shows how many words you've learned.

Tip: Swipe left/right on cards during practice!
